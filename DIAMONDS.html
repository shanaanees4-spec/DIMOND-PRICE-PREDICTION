                                                  LOADING DATA

Loading Data This classic dataset contains the prices and other attributes of almost 54,000 diamonds. There are 10 attributes included in the dataset including the target ie. price. Feature description: price price in US dollars ($326--$ 2757)This is the target column containing tags for the features.

The 4 Cs of Diamonds:-

  1. Carat

Represents the weight of the diamond Range: 0.2 to 5.01 1 carat = 0.2 grams Most objective of all diamond characteristics

  1. Cut

Describes the quality of the diamond’s cut Affects how well the diamond reflects light

Categories: Fair Good Very Good Premium Ideal

  1. Color

Indicates the color quality of the diamond Graded from: D (best, colorless) → J (worst) Colorless diamonds are more valuable 4. Clarity

Measures internal inclusions and external blemishes Categories (worst → best): I1 SI2, SI1 VS2, VS1 VVS2, VVS1 IF Physical Measurements

  1. Depth Total depth percentage Calculated as: (z / mean(x, y)) × 100

  2. Table Width of the top of the diamond relative to its widest point Expressed as a percentage

  3. Dimensions x: Length (mm) y: Width (mm) z: Depth (mm)
